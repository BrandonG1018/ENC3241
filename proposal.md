# Proposal

**Open AI in the against Generative Ai**

**Brandon Ginarte Computer Science**

**Background**

The whole purpose of this study is to research and use ways of combating the use of generative AI for malicious intent using AI. The number of cyber-attacks grows each year. Most of these cyber-attacks come from AI deep fakes and generative images. These scams are popping up increasingly often and without as many people counter them. We can truly use AI to detect and destroy these attacks at a faster rate.

**Methodology**

Data will be collected by a group of student lead team based on the number of fraud accounts, fake news, etc. across social media platforms with AI detection, little AI detection, and no AI detection software.

**Anticipated Outcomes**

The outcome from the research would be records and reports of the heavy AI security would with stand other AI cyber-attacks,

**Significance**

- The purpose of this project is to bring a better understanding on how to protect the cyber community from cyber-attacks using generative AI.
- The general cyber community/everyone would benefit from this project.
- This project will show the community how AI can be used to win this fight against cyber-attacks using generative AI, while also highlighting faults in similar software.
